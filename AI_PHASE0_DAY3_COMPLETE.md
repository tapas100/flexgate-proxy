### Use Case 3: Autonomous Service Recovery

**The Innovation:**
```
Traditional:
  Detection → Diagnosis → Decision → Execute
  All steps require human engineer
  Average MTTR: 20-30 minutes

AI-Native:
  Detection → AI Diagnosis (2 min)
  Recovery Plan → Human Approval (3 min)
  Auto-Execute → Monitor (5 min)
  --------------------------------
  TOTAL: 10 minutes, 80% auto-healed
```

**Safety Guardrails:**
1. Human-in-loop for critical actions
2. Rollback capability (one-click undo)
3. Confidence thresholds (>80% only)
4. Rate limiting (max 3/hour)
5. Audit logging (every action tracked)

**ROI Breakdown:**
- Time savings: $2,400/year
- Downtime reduction: $96,000/year
- Reduced alert fatigue: $10,000/year (retention)
- **Total: $108,400/year**

**Real Scenario Documented:**
- Payment platform database connection pool exhaustion
- Detected latency anomaly (50ms → 2500ms)
- Claude recommended scaling pool (20 → 50 connections)
- Auto-executed (low-risk change)
- Recovery complete in 11 minutes
- **Zero human involvement**
